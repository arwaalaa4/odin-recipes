Maple Glazed Carrots

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup maple syrup
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • freshly chopped parsley for garnish

steps

  1. Place carrots into a pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
  2. Drain and transfer carrots to a serving bowl.
  3. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Stir maple syrup into melted butter and cook until warmed, 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Pour over carrots and toss to coat; season with salt and ground black pepper.
